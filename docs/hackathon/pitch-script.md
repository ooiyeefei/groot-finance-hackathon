# Hackathon Pitch Script — Groot Finance

## Opening (30 seconds)

"What if your finance team never slept, learned from every mistake, and could handle everything from receipt processing to payment — completely autonomously?

Meet Groot — the AI CFO that doesn't just show you dashboards. It **does** your finance work."

## The Problem (30 seconds)

"SMEs spend 15+ hours a week on financial admin. Upload receipts. Create expenses. Wait for approvals. Follow up on payments. Enter journal entries. It's a loop of manual work that drains small teams.

Current tools like Xero and QuickBooks made it digital. But they didn't make it autonomous. You still do all the work — just on a screen."

## The Solution: Autonomous Finance Agent (60 seconds)

**[LIVE DEMO]**

"Let me show you what autonomous looks like."

1. **Voice input**: Enable voice → speak: "I had a forty-five dollar lunch at Sushi Zen for a client meeting"
2. **Agent acts**: Watch the agent create the expense, classify it, extract the data
3. **Auto-approval**: n8n workflow fires → policy check → auto-approved in seconds
4. **Journal entry**: IFRS double-entry created automatically
5. **Voice response**: Agent speaks back the confirmation

"From voice to payment in under 30 seconds. Zero manual steps."

## What Makes This Different (45 seconds)

### 1. Self-Improving AI (DSPy Flywheel)
"When the AI makes a mistake, users correct it. Those corrections train the model weekly. The more you use Groot, the smarter it gets. This is our moat — **compounding intelligence**."

### 2. Agentic Architecture
"42 MCP tools. The agent doesn't just answer questions — it takes action. Creates expenses, approves claims, generates financial reports, forecasts cash flow, detects anomalies."

### 3. Voice-First with MiniMax
"Business owners in Southeast Asia aren't at a desk all day. They're on the floor, in meetings, in their car. Voice-first means they can manage finances hands-free."

### 4. n8n Workflow Automation
"Every financial event triggers an n8n workflow. Submitted expenses auto-route through policy checks, approvals, and payment processing. Humans only intervene for edge cases."

## Traction & Technical Depth (30 seconds)

- **Live in production** at finance.hellogroot.com
- **42 MCP tools** deployed on AWS Lambda
- **DSPy self-improvement** pipeline active (weekly retraining)
- **Mem0 persistent memory** — remembers vendor preferences, team structure
- **IFRS-compliant** double-entry accounting
- **Southeast Asian compliance** — Malaysia LHDN e-invoicing integrated

## The Vision (15 seconds)

"We're not building another accounting app. We're building the AI that replaces the need for one. The Tesla to Xero's Toyota.

Groot Finance — the AI CFO that gets smarter every week."

## Closing

"Thank you. Happy to demo any feature live."
