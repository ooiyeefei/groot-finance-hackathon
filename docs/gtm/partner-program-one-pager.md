## Partner Types

### 1) Referrer (Lead-Only)

You introduce qualified leads. Groot handles demo, close, and onboarding.

**Who can be a Referrer:**
- Accounting firms and bookkeepers
- Business consultants and company secretaries
- Payroll/HR service providers
- **Existing Groot Finance customers** (refer other businesses you know)
- Anyone with a network of SME business owners

**How it works:**
1. Sign up for a partner account (or opt-in from your Groot customer dashboard)
2. Get a unique referral link/code
3. Share with businesses who could benefit from Groot
4. Earn a bounty for every referral that becomes a paying customer

### 2) Reseller (Sell-and-Close)

You source, demo, close deals, and optionally assist with basic onboarding.

**Who should be a Reseller:**
- Accounting firms wanting deeper partner revenue
- IT service providers and software advisors
- Bookkeeping agencies serving multiple SME clients
- Consultants who sell business solutions

**Requirements:**
- Complete Groot Certified Partner training (online, ~2 hours)
- Register deals via partner portal before closing
- Provide basic product walkthrough to customer during sale

## Commercial Rules (Plain English)

### Attribution & Eligibility

- **New logo deals only** — the referred business must not already be a Groot customer or in active Groot sales pipeline
- **90-day attribution window** — from the date you register the lead or share your referral link. If the lead doesn't convert within 90 days, attribution expires
- **First-touch attribution** — if multiple partners refer the same lead, the first registered partner gets credit
- **No self-referrals** — you cannot refer your own business entity
- **Deal registration required (resellers)** — resellers must register the deal via partner portal before or at point of sale. Unregistered deals are not eligible for commission

### Payout Terms

- **Referrer payouts:** Released after the customer completes **30 days** as a paying subscriber (past trial)
- **Reseller close payouts:** Released after the customer completes **60 days** as a paying subscriber
- **Renewal share:** Paid quarterly in arrears (e.g., Q1 renewals paid in April)
- **Clawback:** If a customer churns or requests a refund within 90 days, the close payout is clawed back (deducted from your next payout or invoiced)
- **Monthly payout cycle** for bounties and close payouts; **quarterly settlement** for renewal share

### Commission Basis

- Commission calculated on **net collected subscription revenue** (excluding SST/GST, net of refunds)
- Enterprise commissions calculated on actual contract value (approved case-by-case)
- Credit pack and add-on purchases are **not** commissionable

### Tax & Compliance

- Partners are responsible for reporting commission income under applicable tax laws.
- Groot will provide required commission documentation and apply legally required withholding/reporting where applicable.
- Final tax treatment is subject to current law and advisor guidance.

### Price Transition Plan

| When | Action |
|------|--------|
| **Now** | Create both annual price tiers in Stripe. Use promo prices for checkout during launch window. |
| **Before June 30** | Build annual toggle in billing UI if not already present. Wire up both price IDs. |
| **June 30, 2026** | Archive the promo annual prices in Stripe. New annual checkouts use standard 10% prices only. |
| **On renewal** | Stripe subscription schedule should switch from promo price to standard price. Set up `subscription_schedule` with two phases: Y1 at promo price, Y2+ at standard price. |

### Code Changes Needed

| File | Change |
|------|--------|
| `src/lib/stripe/catalog.ts` | No structural change needed — catalog fetches prices dynamically from Stripe. Set standard annual as `default_price` after June 30, 2026. |
| `src/app/api/v1/billing/checkout/route.ts` | If adding annual toggle: accept `interval` param, look up the correct annual price ID from Stripe. |
| `src/domains/billing/components/pricing-table.tsx` | If adding annual toggle: add monthly/annual switch, compute and display annual savings. |
| Landing page | If showing annual pricing: display savings badge ("Save 10%" or "Save 17% — Launch Special"). |

> **Note:** Since annual prices live entirely in Stripe and the app uses `priceId` for checkout, the main code change is routing users to the correct Stripe price ID based on billing interval selection. No changes to feature flags, limits, or metadata parsing.

## Program Versioning & Roadmap

**Current version: v1 (Launch)**

v1 uses flat close payouts (RM 300 / RM 800) + 5% renewal share. This is intentionally simple to test the channel and learn what motivates partners before committing to a more complex structure.

**v2 considerations (post-launch, based on learnings):**
- Tiered commissions (20-30% percentage-based) if flat payouts prove insufficient for partner recruitment
- Quarterly accelerators / kickers for high-volume partners
- Monthly deal eligibility (currently annual-only)
- Partner dashboard with real-time deal tracking and payout visibility
- Automated CP58 generation for partners exceeding RM 5,000/year
- Extended partner-exclusive promos for seasonal campaigns

Commission rates can always be increased ("we're making the program more generous") — decreasing is harder. Start conservative, scale with data.
