## 3. Partner Types

### A) Referrer (Lead-Only)

Referrer introduces a qualified lead. Groot handles demo, close, and onboarding.

**Who can be a Referrer:**
- Accounting firms and bookkeepers
- Business consultants and company secretaries
- Payroll/HR service providers
- **Existing Groot Finance customers** (refer other businesses you know)
- Anyone with a network of SME business owners

**How it works:**
1. Sign up for a partner account (or opt-in from your Groot customer dashboard)
2. Get a unique referral link/code
3. Share with businesses who could benefit from Groot
4. Earn a bounty for every referral that becomes a paying customer

### B) Reseller (Sell-and-Close)

Reseller sources lead, runs demo, drives close, and provides basic onboarding handoff.

**Who should be a Reseller:**
- Accounting firms wanting deeper partner revenue
- IT service providers and software advisors
- Bookkeeping agencies serving multiple SME clients
- Consultants who sell business solutions

**Requirements:**
- Complete Groot Certified Partner training (online, ~2 hours)
- Register deals via partner portal before closing
- Provide basic product walkthrough to customer during sale

## 7. Commercial Rules

### Attribution & Eligibility

- **New paying subscriptions only** — the referred business must not already be an active paying Groot customer or in active Groot sales pipeline
- **90-day attribution window** — from the date you register the lead or share your referral link
- **First-touch attribution** — if multiple partners refer the same lead, the first registered partner gets credit
- **No self-referrals** — you cannot refer your own business entity
- **Deal registration required (resellers)** — resellers must register via partner portal before or at point of sale

### Payout Terms

- **Referrer payouts:** Released after 30 days as a paying subscriber
- **Reseller close payouts:** Released after 30 days as a paying subscriber
- **Renewal share:** Paid quarterly in arrears (e.g., Q1 renewals paid in April)
- **Clawback:** If a customer churns or requests a refund within 90 days, the close payout is clawed back
- **Downgrade:** If a customer downgrades within 90 days (e.g. Pro to Starter), payout adjusted to lower plan level. After 90 days, original payout is kept
- **Monthly payout cycle** for bounties and close payouts; **quarterly settlement** for renewal share

### Commission Basis

- Calculated on **net collected subscription revenue** (excluding SST/GST, net of refunds)
- Enterprise commissions calculated on actual contract value (approved case-by-case)
- Credit pack and add-on purchases are **not** commissionable

### Tax & Compliance

- Partners are responsible for reporting commission income under applicable tax laws
- Groot will provide required commission documentation and apply legally required withholding/reporting where applicable
- Under Malaysia Income Tax Act Section 83A(1), companies providing >RM 5,000 in monetary/non-monetary incentives to agents must provide Form CP58 by March 31 each year

## 14. Program Versioning & Roadmap

**Current version: v1 (Launch)**

v1 uses flat close payouts (RM 300 / RM 800) + 5% renewal share. Intentionally simple to test the channel and learn what motivates partners before committing to a more complex structure.

**v2 considerations (post-launch, based on learnings):**
- Tiered commissions (20–30% percentage-based) if flat payouts prove insufficient
- Quarterly accelerators / kickers for high-volume partners
- Monthly deal eligibility (currently annual-only)
- Partner dashboard with real-time deal tracking and payout visibility
- Automated CP58 generation for partners exceeding RM 5,000/year
- White-label option for Enterprise resellers
- Revenue share on add-ons (20% of credit pack upsells)
- Annual partner summit
- Extended partner-exclusive promos for seasonal campaigns

Commission rates can always be increased ("we're making the program more generous") — decreasing is harder. Start conservative, scale with data.

## 15. Decision Log (v1)

- Referral reward set at **RM 80** base (not RM 100 — keeps CAC controlled for lead-only motion)
- Starter reseller close payout kept at **RM 300** (11.16% effective rate, aligned with industry standard for one-time payouts)
- Added **5% renewals** from Y2–Y6 (5-year cap) to improve long-term partner motivation and retention alignment
- Added **quarterly accelerators** for both referrer and reseller motions
- Added **plan-scaled payouts** (Pro: RM 800, Enterprise: 10–15%) to prevent Starter-only skew
- Added commercial guardrails: 30-day payout hold, 90-day attribution window, 90-day clawback
- Launch promo (17% off annual) with partner-exclusive extension to Aug 31, 2026
- Upgrade bonuses added for both referrer (+RM 120) and reseller (+RM 500) on Starter-to-Pro upgrades
