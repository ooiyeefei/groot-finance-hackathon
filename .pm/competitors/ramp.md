## Pricing Model

- **Free tier** for corporate card users
- Premium features require card usage
- Revenue from interchange fees + premium subscriptions

---

## Strengths vs FinanSEAL

1. **Corporate Card Integration** - Seamless card-to-expense flow
2. **Massive Integrations Ecosystem** - 200+ apps
3. **US Market Dominance** - Strong brand recognition
4. **Fast Reimbursements** - 2-day turnaround

## Weaknesses vs FinanSEAL

1. **No SEA Focus** - Limited SEA currency/tax support
2. **Card-Centric Model** - Requires using their cards
3. **US-Centric Compliance** - No GST/SST/VAT for SEA
4. **Enterprise Pricing** - May be expensive for micro-SMEs
