## Strengths vs FinanSEAL

1. **Bank Feeds** - Direct bank connections
2. **Full Accounting** - Complete double-entry system
3. **App Ecosystem** - 1,000+ integrations
4. **Accountant Network** - Strong professional partnerships
5. **SEA Presence** - Singapore & Malaysia offices
6. **Tax Compliance** - GST/SST ready reports

## Weaknesses vs FinanSEAL

1. **Expense as Add-on** - Not core product (S$5/user extra)
2. **Generic OCR** - Not SEA-optimized (Hubdoc is Western-focused)
3. **Complex for Non-Accountants** - Accounting knowledge required
4. **No AI Categorization** - Manual category assignment
5. **Price per User** - Gets expensive with team growth
