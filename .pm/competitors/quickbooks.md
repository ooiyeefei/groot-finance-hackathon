## Strengths vs FinanSEAL

1. **Full Accounting Suite** - Complete double-entry accounting
2. **Bank Feeds** - Automatic transaction import
3. **Massive Ecosystem** - 750+ app integrations
4. **Accountant Network** - Strong accountant partnerships
5. **Brand Recognition** - Trusted, established brand

## Weaknesses vs FinanSEAL

1. **Basic OCR** - Generic, not SEA-optimized
2. **Manual Categorization** - Limited AI assistance
3. **Complex UX** - Steep learning curve for non-accountants
4. **Western-Centric** - Limited SEA localization
5. **No Approval Workflows** - Requires add-ons for expense approval
