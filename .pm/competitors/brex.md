## Pricing Model

- Free for card users
- Premium tiers for advanced features
- Revenue from interchange + deposits + premium

---

## Strengths vs FinanSEAL

1. **Card-Native Experience** - Seamless card-to-books flow
2. **Global Reach** - 120+ countries
3. **Enterprise Scale** - Proven at large scale
4. **24/7 Support** - Always-on customer service

## Weaknesses vs FinanSEAL

1. **No SEA Specialization** - Generic global approach
2. **Card Lock-in** - Must use Brex cards
3. **US-Centric** - Limited local tax compliance
4. **Not for Non-Card Users** - Less useful without cards
