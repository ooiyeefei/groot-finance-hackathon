## Strengths vs FinanSEAL

1. **Free Tier** - Generous free plan
2. **Zoho Ecosystem** - 50+ integrated apps
3. **Zia AI** - Built-in AI assistant
4. **Emerging Market Focus** - Good India/SEA presence
5. **Affordable Pricing** - Very competitive pricing

## Weaknesses vs FinanSEAL

1. **Basic OCR** - Not SEA-optimized
2. **Complex Setup** - Many options can overwhelm
3. **Suite Lock-in** - Best with other Zoho products
4. **Support Quality** - Mixed reviews on support
5. **UI/UX** - Dated interface compared to modern tools
