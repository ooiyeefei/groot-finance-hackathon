**Why WAIT:** Current post-facto approval works; pre-approval is enhancement

**Scope:** Spend request submission, manager pre-approval, virtual approval limits

### GAP 8: Vendor Price Tracking UI (Score: 36)

**Scope:** Vendor list, price history charts, alerts

### GAP 9: Budget Management & Alerts (Score: 35)
**Competitor Evidence:** Xero, QuickBooks, Zoho have budgeting

**Why WAIT:** User rated "Medium" priority

### GAP 10: Bank Statement Import (Score: 32)

### GAP 11: Cash Flow Forecasting (Score: 30)
**Competitor Evidence:** Zoho has predictive cash flow

**Trigger:** User feedback on needing sales invoice generation
